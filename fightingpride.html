Fighting Pride - Pacquiao Saga

Summary

Currently in development under Ranida Games that is on live beta in the PlayStore exclusively in the Philippines.

Fighting Pride is a beat-’em-up sports-adventure game featuring an action-packed gameplay and an adaptation of Manny Pacquiao’s inspiring life story.

Key parts of the storyline are represented by boxing matches from Manny’s amateur days up to his professional career, showcasing the harsh realities he faced while rising from poverty and as he grows into the successful boxing legend that he is today!

What I did

  • Gameplay prototyping.
  • Gameplay UI implementations and functionalities.
  • Tools development for level editor and enemy management.
  • Multiplayer design and prototyping.
