Waaaaah! Shout Your Lungs Out!

Summary

Published by Ranida Games for Android.

Waaaah! Shout Your Lungs Out! is a simple yet fun mobile game where you let out your emotions through screaming at your mobile phone.

Challenge yourself or compete with others to see who will scream the loudest, longest, or even rage the most! Play different game modes where your vocal cords will be put to the test and don't forget to shout your lungs out!

What I did

  • Gameplay prototyping and development initial release.
  • UI implementations and functionalities.
  • Implemented In-App Purchase, AdMob ads, and Unity ads.
